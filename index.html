About Daisy's Wardrobe


Daisy's Wardrobe is a fashion brand dedicated to celebrating individuality and self-expression.
Founded by Sakshi Kumari, our brand offers a curated collection that embraces diversity and
empowers confidence. We prioritize sustainable practices, crafting garments that blend comfort,
quality, and innovative design. Our mission is to redefine fashion by creating inclusive, stylish
pieces that reflect authenticity and sophistication. Join us in exploring our fashion-forward,
eco-conscious brand that values individuality and timeless elegance.
